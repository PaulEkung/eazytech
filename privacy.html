1. Introduction

Welcome to Eazytech. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may provide while navigating our website. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect and how we manage it.

Because this website serves as a static information platform, we do not require a user account configuration or a backend database layer. However, data may be gathered via the following methods:

  • Server Logs & Analytics: Like most websites, our hosting infrastructure may automatically log basic network requests (such as your IP address, browser type, and timestamps) to maintain platform stability and performance.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We may use basic cookies or local browser storage to optimize user configuration settings and analyze general traffic trends. You can instruct your web browser to refuse all cookies or to notify you when a cookie is being transmitted; however, some sections of our platform may require them to function optimally.

4. Third-Party Services

Our infrastructure works in tandem with third-party service providers (such as hosting nodes, external fonts, and content delivery networks) to serve files smoothly. These external systems access data solely to perform technical tasks on our behalf and are restricted from disclosing or utilizing it for any alternative marketing operations.

5. Data Security

The security of your informational data is paramount to us. We employ standard web security practices to protect details sent to our network. However, please remember that no structural transmission method over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute absolute containment.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or replace this policy text at any time. Any strategic updates will be posted transparently on this page with an adjusted revision date. We encourage visitors to check back periodically to review changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or clarifications regarding this architectural privacy setup, please feel free to reach out to our administration team directly via our primary organizational channels.
